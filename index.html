About Metrics

WPM (Words Per Minute): Number of correct characters typed divided by 5, scaled per minute.

CPM (Characters Per Minute): Number of correct characters typed per minute.

Accuracy: Percentage of correct characters out of all characters you typed.

Errors: Count of characters that don’t match the prompt (including extra characters beyond the prompt).
